   # Privacy Policy for vPlayer HiFi

   **Last updated: June 2025**

   vPlayer HiFi does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

   - **No Account Required.** The app does not require sign-in or registration.
   - **No Data Collection.** We do not collect usage data, crash reports, or analytics.
   - **No Network Access.** The app functions entirely offline. It does not connect to the
 internet.
   - **No Third-Party Services.** No advertising, tracking, or analytics SDKs are included.
   - **Your Music Stays Yours.** Audio files are stored locally on your device and are never
 accessed, uploaded, or shared.
